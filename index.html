Basic Injury Care & Recovery Tips

If you get a minor injury during workout or daily activity, follow these simple steps to recover safely:

🧊 Rest & Ice

Rest the injured area and apply ice for 15–20 minutes to reduce swelling and pain.

🩹 Compression

Use a light bandage to support the injured area and avoid extra movement.

⬆️ Elevation

Keep the injured part elevated above heart level to reduce swelling.

⚠️ When to See Doctor

If pain is severe, swelling increases, or movement becomes difficult, consult a doctor immediately.
